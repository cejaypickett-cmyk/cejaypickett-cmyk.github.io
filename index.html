What we're building

One platform to see your brewery's full financial picture

Live data connections

Pull directly from Xero, Breww, SAP, Lightspeed, and more. No manual exports or spreadsheets.

Brewery-specific dashboards

P&L by product line, taproom vs distribution, cost per litre, duty tracking — built for how breweries actually operate.

Your data stays yours

Fully isolated client data with enterprise-grade security. Each brewery sees only their own numbers.

Refreshed throughout the day

Data syncs automatically up to 8 times daily so your dashboards always reflect reality, not last month's numbers.

BrewView is being built by someone who's spent 5+ years building financial dashboards inside a brewing company. We saw first-hand how hard it is to get a clear picture when your data lives across Xero, Breww, spreadsheets, and POS systems. So we're building the tool we always wished existed.
